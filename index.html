Main Architecture of the Management System

An integrated operational engine built to control field logistics, workforce alignment, and financial accountability.

1. Project Management

  • Create and manage construction projects
  • Track project progress systematically
  • Set project deadlines and milestones
  • Monitor project status in real time

2. Task Management

  • Assign tasks to engineers, supervisors, and workers
  • Track individual task completion
  • Set priorities and operational deadlines
  • Receive instantaneous task updates

3. Employee & Labor

  • Maintain clear worker profile records
  • Track on-site daily attendance
  • Manage complex salaries and payroll
  • Assign workers to specific physical sites

4. Material Management

  • Track active construction materials
  • Monitor real-time warehouse stock levels
  • Request and approve material purchases
  • Generate detailed inventory reports

5. Equipment Management

  • Manage heavy machinery and field equipment
  • Track variable equipment field usage
  • Schedule routine preventive maintenance
  • Record active equipment availability logs

6. Budget & Cost

  • Create functional project cost budgets
  • Track variable expenditures and expenses
  • Compare estimated vs actual structural costs
  • Generate comprehensive financial reports

7. Subcontractor & Supplier

  • Store external contractor information
  • Manage primary supplier contact profiles
  • Track formal contracts and vendor agreements
  • Monitor overall supplier delivery performance

8. Document Management

  • Upload structural drawings and CAD blueprints
  • Store legal contracts and municipal permits
  • Manage project documentation securely
  • Download and share active files instantly

9. Site Monitoring

  • Compile field-driven daily site logs
  • Upload real visual progress photos
  • Log regulatory safety inspection records
  • Track structural site incidents and issues

10. Communication

  • Utilize localized, direct team messaging
  • Deploy push notifications and critical alerts
  • Broadcast urgent broad-team announcements
  • Schedule engineering and planning meetings

11. Reports & Analytics

  • Export structural milestone reports
  • Analyze actual vs projected budget reports
  • Track raw material depletion reports
  • Interact with dynamic data chart visuals

12. Client Portal

  • Enable stakeholder visual tracking entry
  • Offer access to verified field invoice summaries
  • Communicate directly with site managers
  • Monitor upcoming construction targets
